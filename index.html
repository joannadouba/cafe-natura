We are Cafe Natura

Humans were never meant to live between four walls separate from nature. At Cafe Natura, we recognize and celebrate that, which is why we invite you to join us in our sunroom, filled with trees and plants of all kinds, shapes, and sizes. Enjoy our locally-sourced coffee under a tree, while sitting on hand-crafted chairs designed and produced by local furniture makers. Cafe Natura is the perfect spot for a break from your busy schedule.
