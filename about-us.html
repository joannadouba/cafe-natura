About Us

Concept

Humans were never meant to live between four walls separate from nature. At Cafe Natura, we recognize and celebrate that, which is why we invite you to join us in our sunroom, filled with trees and plants of all kinds, shapes, and sizes. Enjoy our locally-sourced coffee under a tree, while sitting on hand-crafted chairs designed and produced by local furniture makers. Cafe Natura is the perfect spot for a break from your busy schedule.

Founders

Abed is an IT Solutions Architect by day, superman by night. He loves the outdoors and meditating in nature. His background is in computer science and technical sales, but his heart belongs to photography and cooking.

Joanna is Abed's other half. Although her background is in graphic design, she worked in animation for most of her career before founding Cafe Natura with Abed. She enjoys photography as well as eating the food Abed cooks.
